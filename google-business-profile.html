Google Business Profile FAQ

How long does it take to see results?

Initial improvements can be seen within 2-4 weeks, with significant results typically appearing within 2-3 months as Google recognizes the optimization efforts.

Do you handle negative reviews?

Yes, we monitor all reviews and provide professional responses to both positive and negative feedback. We also help implement strategies to generate more positive reviews.

What if I don't have a Google Business Profile yet?

No problem! We'll create and verify your Google Business Profile from scratch, ensuring it's properly set up and optimized from day one.

How often do you post to my Google Business Profile?

We create and publish weekly Google posts featuring new properties, market insights, success stories, and calls-to-action to keep your profile active and engaging.

Will this help me rank higher on Google?

Absolutely! Google Business Profile optimization is a key component of local SEO. A well-optimized profile significantly improves your chances of appearing in local search results and the Google Map Pack.

Do you provide analytics and reporting?

Yes, we provide monthly reports showing your Google Business Profile performance, including views, clicks, calls, direction requests, and search visibility improvements.
