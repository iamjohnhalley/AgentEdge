Frequently Asked Questions

What's the catch with performance-pay?

There's no catch. We're confident in our ability to generate results, so we put our money where our mouth is. If we don't deliver at least one qualified valuation lead in your first 30 days, you don't pay for that month. It's that simple.

How does county exclusivity work?

We only work with one independent estate agent per county on a first-come, first-served basis. This means you'll never compete with another AgentEdge client in your local market, giving you a significant advantage.

Can I really cancel anytime?

Absolutely. We require only 30 days notice to cancel. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. We earn your business every month by delivering results, not by locking you into agreements.

What counts as a qualified valuation lead?

A qualified lead is someone who contacts you specifically requesting a property valuation, has a genuine property to value, and provides valid contact information. We track these through your website forms, phone calls, and social media inquiries.

What if my county is already taken?

If your primary county is taken, we'll work with you to identify adjacent counties or areas where you'd like to expand. We can also put you on a waiting list if the current agent in your county decides to leave.

Do you work with franchise agents?

We specialize in independent estate agents who need the marketing edge to compete with larger franchises. Our strategies are specifically designed for independent agents in rural Irish counties.
