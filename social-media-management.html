Social Media Management FAQ

Do I need to provide content, or do you create everything?

We create all content for you! You just need to provide property photos/videos, and we handle all the writing, editing, scheduling, and posting.

How do you create property videos if I only have photos?

We can create engaging slideshow videos from your property photos, or we can edit raw video footage you provide. We add music, text overlays, and professional transitions.

Will you respond to comments and messages?

Yes! We monitor and respond to all comments and direct messages professionally. Any potential leads are immediately forwarded to you for follow-up.

Can I approve posts before they go live?

Absolutely! We can set up an approval process where you review and approve all content before it's published, or you can give us full posting authority.

What if I don't have business accounts set up?

No problem! We'll help you set up professional business accounts on all platforms, optimize them with your branding, and ensure they're properly configured for maximum visibility.

How do you measure success?

We track engagement rates, follower growth, reach, website clicks, and most importantly - leads generated. You'll receive monthly reports showing all key metrics and ROI.
