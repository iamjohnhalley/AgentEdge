About AgentEdge

Founded by real estate marketing experts with 8+ years of experience helping independent agents compete and win in rural Irish markets.

Our Story

AgentEdge was born from a simple observation: independent estate agents in rural Ireland were being outmarketed by larger franchises, despite having deeper local knowledge and better personal service.

After spending over 8 years in real estate marketing, we saw talented agents losing business not because they weren't good at their job, but because they couldn't compete with the professional marketing presence of bigger players.

We realized that most independent agents faced the same challenges:

  • Outdated or non-existent websites
  • Inconsistent social media presence
  • Poor local search visibility
  • No budget for hiring marketing staff
  • Lack of time to learn digital marketing

That's when we decided to create AgentEdge—a done-for-you marketing solution designed specifically for independent agents in rural counties, with a performance-pay model that eliminates all risk.
