SEO & Content FAQ

How long does it take to see SEO results?

Initial improvements can be seen within 2-3 months, with significant results typically appearing within 4-6 months. SEO is a long-term strategy that builds momentum over time.

Do you write blog posts for my website?

Yes! We create monthly SEO-optimized blog posts specifically for your website, covering local market insights, area guides, and buyer/seller education.

Will this help my Google Business Profile ranking too?

Absolutely! Our SEO work integrates with Google Business Profile optimization, creating a comprehensive local search strategy that improves all your online visibility.

What keywords do you target?

We focus on county-specific keywords like "estate agent [county]", "houses for sale [area]", "property valuation [town]", plus long-tail keywords that your potential clients search for.

Do you guarantee first page rankings?

While no one can guarantee specific rankings, our track record shows consistent improvement in local search visibility. We focus on sustainable, white-hat SEO practices that deliver long-term results.

How do you measure SEO success?

We track keyword rankings, organic traffic growth, website conversions, and most importantly - leads generated from organic search. Monthly reports show all key metrics and progress.
